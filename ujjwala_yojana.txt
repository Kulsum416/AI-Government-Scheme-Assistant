Scheme Name: Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY)

Eligibility:
Women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) households.

Benefits:
Provides free LPG gas connections to promote clean cooking fuel usage.

How to Apply:
Applicants can apply at LPG distribution centers or through local authorities.

Notes:
The scheme helps reduce health issues caused by traditional cooking fuels.
