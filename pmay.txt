Scheme Name: Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)

Eligibility:
Families from Economically Weaker Sections (EWS), Low Income Groups (LIG), and Middle Income Groups (MIG).

Benefits:
Provides financial assistance to eligible families for building or purchasing a permanent house.

How to Apply:
Applicants can apply online through the PMAY official website or through local government offices.

Notes:
The scheme aims to provide affordable housing for all.
