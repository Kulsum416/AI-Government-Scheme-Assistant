Scheme Name: PM Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Eligibility:
Small and marginal farmers who own cultivable agricultural land in India.

Benefits:
Eligible farmers receive financial assistance of Rs. 6000 per year. 
The amount is paid in three equal installments directly to the bank account.

How to Apply:
Farmers can register themselves on the PM-KISAN official website.
They can also apply through Common Service Centres (CSCs) or local agriculture offices.

Notes:
The scheme is fully funded by the Government of India.
Farmers must complete Aadhaar verification to receive benefits.
