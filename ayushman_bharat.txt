Scheme Name: Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)

Eligibility:
Economically weaker families identified as per SECC data and other eligible categories.

Benefits:
Provides health insurance coverage up to Rs. 5 lakhs per family per year for secondary and tertiary care.

How to Apply:
Eligible beneficiaries can visit empanelled hospitals or check eligibility on the Ayushman Bharat portal.

Notes:
The scheme covers hospitalization expenses and is implemented across India.
